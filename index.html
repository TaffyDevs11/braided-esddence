About Braided Essence By Yemu

At Braided Essence by Yemu, we believe every braid is a statement of beauty, confidence, and culture. Founded in 2025 by Yemurai Chigubu, our brand offers professional braiding services including box braids, knotless styles, cornrows, twists, and wig installations—for both women and men. Based in Rzeszow, we bring the salon to your doorstep with our premium mobile service, designed for convenience and comfort.

What makes us different? It’s the clean, cozy setup, the unmatched attention to detail, and our commitment to customer care—from snacks to loyalty rewards. We’re not just about hair—we’re about the full experience. With a growing list of happy clients in Rzeszow and Krakow, we’re here to help you look and feel your best, every time..
